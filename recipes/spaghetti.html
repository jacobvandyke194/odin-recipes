Spaghetti

Description:

It just surprises me that they are all different, but I love that everyone has their own twist on this classic dinner recipe. We too have a favorite Spaghetti recipe we’ve made for years and love. That’s probably why we make this Spaghetti for dinner so often (we also have a great Baked Spaghetti and Chicken Spaghetti)! It’s simple, easy to make, always tastes delicious, and everyone likes it. And honestly, leftovers taste exactly the same heated up as if it was just made (my kind of leftover)!!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Ground Beef
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Parmesan Cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil Spaghetti Noodles
  2. Heat Tomato Sauce
  3. Add sauce to pan
  4. Add Cooked Meat to pan
  5. Add Noodles to pan
